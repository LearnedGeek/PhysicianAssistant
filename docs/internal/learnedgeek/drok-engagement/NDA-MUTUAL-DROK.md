## 1. Purpose

The Parties are exploring a business relationship involving the development and deployment of an AI-powered physician triage and patient communication platform (the "Project," referred to as "DrOk"). In connection with this Project, each Party will disclose confidential and proprietary information to the other.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

**From Party A (Learned Geek LLC):** Software, source code, architecture, system design, algorithms, AI prompt engineering, technical specifications, business plans, pricing models, financial data, and project documentation.

**From Party B (Dr. Núñez):** Clinical workflows, patient communication processes, medical domain expertise, clinical protocols, triage criteria, emergency thresholds, professional contacts, business plans, and any information relating to Dr. Núñez's medical practice or the Infanzia franchise.

---

## 3. Obligations

Each Party, when acting as a Receiving Party, agrees to:

3.1 Hold all Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party in strict confidence.

3.2 Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from the Disclosing Party, except as provided in Section 4.

3.3 Use Confidential Information solely for purposes related to the Project.

3.4 Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

3.5 Limit internal access to Confidential Information to those individuals who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those herein.

---

## 4. Permitted Disclosures

4.1 Each Party may disclose Confidential Information to its legal counsel for the purpose of obtaining legal advice related to the Project.

4.2 Party B may disclose Confidential Information to Carlos Rojas (Rebaza, Alcázar & De Las Casas) for regulatory and legal review related to the Project, provided such counsel is bound by professional confidentiality obligations.

4.3 Party A may disclose Confidential Information to legal counsel retained for contract or regulatory review related to the Project, provided such counsel is bound by professional confidentiality obligations.

---

## 5. Exclusions

Obligations do not apply to information that:

5.1 Is or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party.

5.2 Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records.

5.3 Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information.

5.4 Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.

5.5 Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party before disclosure and reasonably cooperates with any effort to obtain protective treatment.

---

## 6. Return or Destruction

Upon request or termination of the relationship, each Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information received from the other Party and certify such destruction in writing. Notwithstanding the foregoing, each Party may retain copies required by law, regulation, or professional obligation, provided such copies remain subject to the confidentiality obligations herein.

---

## 7. No License or Commitment

7.1 Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any license, right, or interest in the other Party's intellectual property.

7.2 Nothing in this Agreement obligates either Party to proceed with any business relationship, transaction, contract, or the Project itself.

---

## 8. Term

This Agreement is effective upon execution and the confidentiality obligations remain in force for three (3) years from the date of disclosure of each item of Confidential Information.

---

## 9. Remedies

Each Party acknowledges that breach of this Agreement could cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate. The non-breaching Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief without posting bond, in addition to all other remedies available at law or equity.

---

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin. Disputes shall be resolved in Waukesha County, Wisconsin, unless both Parties agree in writing to an alternative forum.

---

## 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding confidentiality related to the Project and supersedes all prior discussions on this topic. This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both Parties.
