# Exclusivity & License Addendum
## DrOk Project — Addendum to Master Services Agreement

**Between:**
- **Provider:** Learned Geek LLC
- **Client:** Dr. Martín Núñez

**Addendum Effective Date:** _______________

---

This Exclusivity & License Addendum ("Addendum") supplements the Master Services Agreement ("MSA") and defines the license terms, exclusivity period, and platform fee structure for the DrOk platform.

---

## 1. Platform Ownership

1.1 Learned Geek LLC retains sole and exclusive ownership of the DrOk platform, including all software, code, architecture, AI prompts, system designs, documentation, and derivative works, in perpetuity. This is consistent with and reinforces MSA Section 2.

1.2 "Platform" means the complete AI-powered physician triage and patient communication system, including but not limited to: the conversational AI engine, RAG pipeline, physician dashboard, admin portal, emergency detection system, and all supporting infrastructure.

---

## 2. Brand Ownership

2.1 Client retains ownership of the "DrOk" brand name, trademarks, and associated brand assets.

2.2 Provider shall not use the DrOk brand for marketing, sublicensing, or white-label purposes without Client's prior written consent.

2.3 Client shall not represent the platform as Client's proprietary technology. All representations to third parties shall accurately describe the platform as licensed from Learned Geek LLC.

---

## 3. Exclusive License

3.1 Provider grants Client an **exclusive license** to use the platform under the DrOk brand for the purpose of providing AI-assisted physician triage services.

3.2 **Exclusivity Period:** Twenty-four (24) months from the date the platform enters production use ("Go-Live Date").

3.3 During the Exclusivity Period, Provider shall not license the platform to any third party for the same use case (AI-powered physician triage and patient communication) without Client's written consent.

3.4 Provider retains the right during the Exclusivity Period to:
- Use the platform technology in non-competing applications (e.g., non-medical AI systems)
- Continue development of the underlying technology
- Publish research based on anonymized, non-identifiable architectural findings

---

## 4. Post-Exclusivity

4.1 Upon expiration of the Exclusivity Period, the license automatically converts to a **non-exclusive license** on the same commercial terms.

4.2 Provider may license the platform to additional parties under different brand names.

4.3 Client retains the right to continue using the platform under the DrOk brand under the same terms.

4.4 Client may request an extension of exclusivity by written notice at least ninety (90) days before expiration. Extension terms shall be negotiated in good faith and may include adjusted commercial terms.

---

## 5. Revenue Share

5.1 The Parties agree to the following revenue share structure:

| Revenue Type | Provider (Learned Geek) | Client (DrOk) |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly per-physician platform fee | 60% | 40% |
| One-time physician onboarding fee | 20% | 80% |
| Parent/patient subscription (future B2C) | 40% | 60% |

5.2 Revenue is calculated on gross receipts before taxes and payment processing fees.

5.3 Client shall provide Provider with a monthly revenue report within fifteen (15) days of each month's end, detailing: number of active physicians, fees collected per physician, and any onboarding or subscription fees collected.

5.4 Provider's share shall be remitted within thirty (30) days of each month's end.

---

## 6. Upfront Build Fee

6.1 Client shall pay Provider a reduced upfront build fee to offset development costs during the build phase. The build fee and payment schedule are specified in the applicable Statement of Work.

6.2 The build fee is a services fee, not an IP purchase or equity investment. It does not convey any ownership interest in the platform.

---

## 7. Minimum Performance

7.1 To maintain exclusivity, Client shall use commercially reasonable efforts to market and sell the platform to additional physicians.

7.2 If, at the twelve (12) month mark following Go-Live, fewer than five (5) physicians (including Client) are actively using the platform, Provider may, at its sole discretion, convert the license to non-exclusive with sixty (60) days written notice.

7.3 This provision is intended to ensure mutual benefit — not to penalize Client for market conditions outside their control. The Parties agree to discuss performance in good faith before any conversion is triggered.

---

## 8. Right of First Refusal

8.1 If either Party receives a bona fide offer from a third party to acquire the platform (Provider) or the DrOk brand/business (Client), the other Party shall have a right of first refusal to match the offer within thirty (30) days of written notification.

8.2 This right survives termination of the MSA for a period of two (2) years.

---

## 9. Termination Effects

9.1 If Client terminates the MSA or this Addendum, Client's license to the platform terminates. Client shall cease use of the platform within thirty (30) days.

9.2 If Provider terminates the MSA or this Addendum, Client's license continues for a transition period of six (6) months to allow Client to migrate to an alternative solution.

9.3 Revenue share obligations continue for any period during which Client is using the platform.
