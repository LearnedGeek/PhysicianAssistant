# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement
## Learned Geek LLC

**Entity ID:** L087476 | **EIN:** 41-3648316

---

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Learned Geek LLC, a Wisconsin Limited Liability Company ("Party A"), and the undersigned ("Party B"), effective as of the date signed below. Party A and Party B are each referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

---

## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose confidential and proprietary information to the other.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, whether orally, in writing, or by inspection, including but not limited to: software, source code, architecture, business plans, client information, financial data, technical specifications, project scope, pricing, clinical processes, medical workflows, patient data schemas, and any information designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the context.

---

## 3. Obligations

Each Party, when acting as a Receiving Party, agrees to:

3.1 Hold all Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party in strict confidence.

3.2 Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from the Disclosing Party.

3.3 Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose stated above.

3.4 Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

3.5 Limit internal access to Confidential Information to those individuals who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those herein.

---

## 4. Exclusions

Obligations do not apply to information that:

4.1 Is or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party.

4.2 Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records.

4.3 Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information.

4.4 Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.

4.5 Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party before disclosure and reasonably cooperates with any effort to obtain protective treatment.

---

## 5. Return or Destruction

Upon request or termination of the relationship, each Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information received from the other Party and certify such destruction in writing. Notwithstanding the foregoing, each Party may retain copies required by law, regulation, or internal compliance policy, provided such copies remain subject to the confidentiality obligations herein.

---

## 6. No License

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any license, right, or interest in the other Party's intellectual property.

---

## 7. No Obligation

Nothing in this Agreement obligates either Party to proceed with any business relationship, transaction, or contract beyond the Purpose stated herein.

---

## 8. Term

This Agreement is effective upon execution and the confidentiality obligations remain in force for three (3) years from the date of disclosure of each item of Confidential Information.

---

## 9. Remedies

Each Party acknowledges that breach of this Agreement could cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate. The non-breaching Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief without posting bond, in addition to all other remedies available at law or equity.

---

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin. Disputes shall be resolved in Waukesha County, Wisconsin.

---

## 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding confidentiality and supersedes all prior discussions on this topic. This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both Parties.
