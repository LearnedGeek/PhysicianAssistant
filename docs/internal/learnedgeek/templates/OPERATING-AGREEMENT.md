## Article 1 — Formation

1.1 **Name.** The name of the limited liability company is Learned Geek LLC ("Company").

1.2 **Principal Office.** N61W35661 Bayshore Circle, Oconomowoc, WI 53066.

1.3 **Registered Agent.** Mark M. McArthey, at the address stated above.

1.4 **Purpose.** Custom software development, API integration, consulting, workshops, seminars, and related technology services.

1.5 **Term.** Perpetual unless dissolved per Wisconsin law or this Agreement.

---

## Article 2 — Member

2.1 **Sole Member.** Mark M. McArthey holds 100% membership interest.

2.2 **Additional Members.** No additional members may be admitted without written amendment signed by the existing Member.

---

## Article 3 — Management

3.1 **Member-Managed.** The Member has full authority to bind the Company, enter contracts, open accounts, and make all decisions.

3.2 **Delegation.** The Member may delegate authority by written authorization but retains ultimate management authority.

---

## Article 4 — Capital and Contributions

4.1 **Initial Contribution.** As recorded in the Company's books.

4.2 **Additional Contributions.** Not required but permitted at Member's discretion.

4.3 **No Interest.** No interest shall be paid on capital contributions.

---

## Article 5 — Distributions and Compensation

5.1 **Distributions.** At such times and amounts as the Member determines, provided the Company remains solvent.

5.2 **Member Compensation.** The Member may receive reasonable compensation for services rendered.

5.3 **Tax Treatment.** Treated as a disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes unless otherwise elected. Member reports income and losses on personal return.

---

## Article 6 — Intellectual Property

6.1 **Company Ownership.** All IP created by the Member or any contractor, employee, or intern in the course of Company business — including software, code, documentation, designs, and derivative works — is the sole property of Learned Geek LLC in perpetuity.

6.2 **Pre-Existing IP.** IP created prior to formation and contributed to the Company shall be documented in a separate IP assignment schedule.

6.3 **Contractor Assignments.** Any contractor, subcontractor, or intern must execute a written IP assignment agreement before performing any work.

---

## Article 7 — Liability and Indemnification

7.1 **Limited Liability.** The Member shall not be personally liable for Company debts solely by reason of being a Member.

7.2 **Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify the Member against claims arising from Company business, provided the Member acted in good faith.

7.3 **Veil Preservation.** To preserve limited liability: maintain a separate business bank account, do not commingle personal and business funds, and maintain adequate Company records.

---

## Article 8 — Dissolution

8.1 **Voluntary Dissolution.** The Member may dissolve at any time by filing Articles of Dissolution with the Wisconsin DFI.

8.2 **Winding Up.** Pay or provide for all liabilities; distribute remaining assets to the Member.

---

## Article 9 — Miscellaneous

9.1 **Governing Law.** State of Wisconsin, Chapter 183, Wis. Stats.

9.2 **Amendments.** Written instrument signed by the Member only.

9.3 **Entire Agreement.** Supersedes all prior understandings.

9.4 **Severability.** Invalid provisions do not affect remaining provisions.
