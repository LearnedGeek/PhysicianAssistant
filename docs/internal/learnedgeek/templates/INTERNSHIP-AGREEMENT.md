# Internship Agreement
## Learned Geek LLC

**Entity ID:** L087476 | **EIN:** 41-3648316

---

This Internship Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Learned Geek LLC, a Wisconsin Limited Liability Company ("Company"), and the undersigned intern ("Intern"), effective as of the date signed below.

---

## 1. Internship Description

1.1 **Position.** Intern will serve as a Web & Software Development Intern under the supervision of Mark M. McArthey.

1.2 **Duration.** Start Date: June 8, 2026 | Anticipated End Date: July 31, 2026

1.3 **Hours.** Minimum 72 hours, maximum 144 hours over the internship period. Approximately 18 hours per week, by mutual agreement. For unpaid internships, Intern shall not exceed 144 total hours.

1.4 **Location.** Hybrid — primarily remote, with occasional in-person meetings in the Oconomowoc, WI area.

---

## 2. Educational Nature

2.1 This internship is structured to provide training and experience similar to an educational environment and is primarily for the benefit of the Intern, consistent with the primary beneficiary test under the Fair Labor Standards Act (29 C.F.R. § 553) and applicable Wisconsin labor standards.

2.2 This internship has been coordinated with Waukesha County Technical College ("WCTC") and satisfies the requirements of the WCTC IT Web & Software Developer internship program.

2.3 The Intern does not displace regular employees of the Company.

2.4 **WCTC Program Requirements.** The following are required by the WCTC internship program and are incorporated into this Agreement:
- Intern and Supervisor shall collaborate to establish 3–5 SMART goals within the first week.
- Supervisor shall complete a mid-term evaluation and a final evaluation, as provided by WCTC.
- Supervisor shall meet with the WCTC Internship Course Instructor at least once during the semester.
- Intern shall complete a WCTC Training Agreement prior to the start of the internship.

---

## 3. Compensation

This is an unpaid internship. Intern acknowledges that no wages, salary, or other compensation will be paid by the Company for services rendered under this Agreement.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property Assignment

4.1 **Assignment.** Intern hereby assigns to Learned Geek LLC all right, title, and interest in any and all work product, code, software, documentation, designs, inventions, and other materials created by Intern during the internship, whether or not created during regular working hours ("Work Product").

4.2 **Work Made for Hire.** To the extent permitted by law, all Work Product constitutes works made for hire belonging to the Company. To the extent any Work Product does not qualify as a work made for hire, Intern irrevocably assigns all rights to the Company.

4.3 **Assistance.** Intern agrees to execute any documents reasonably requested by the Company to perfect or record the Company's ownership of Work Product.

4.4 **Pre-Existing IP.** Intern shall disclose any pre-existing IP that may be incorporated into Work Product. Such pre-existing IP is not assigned under this Agreement unless separately agreed in writing.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1 Intern agrees to keep confidential all non-public information about the Company, its clients, projects, and technology encountered during the internship.

5.2 This obligation survives the end of the internship for two (2) years.

5.3 Intern shall not use confidential information for any purpose other than performing internship duties.

5.4 If the Parties have executed a separate Non-Disclosure Agreement, the terms of that NDA shall supplement this Section 5.

---

## 6. Data Access and Restrictions

6.1 **No Access to Protected Health Information.** Intern shall not be granted access to, nor shall Intern seek access to, any real patient data, protected health information (PHI), personally identifiable information (PII) of end users, or production databases containing such data.

6.2 **Test Data Only.** All development and testing performed by Intern shall use synthetic, anonymized, or mock data provided by the Company.

6.3 **Reporting Obligation.** If Intern inadvertently encounters real patient data or PII during the course of work, Intern shall immediately notify the Supervisor and cease any activity involving such data.

6.4 **No Data Removal.** Intern shall not copy, download, export, or transfer any Company data — including test data — to personal devices, personal cloud storage, or any system not authorized by the Company.

---

## 7. Tools, Accounts, and Access

7.1 The Company may provide Intern with access to development tools, repositories, cloud services, and communication platforms necessary to perform internship duties.

7.2 All accounts and access credentials provided to Intern remain the property of the Company.

7.3 **Access Termination.** Upon completion or termination of the internship, all access to Company systems shall be revoked within 24 hours, including but not limited to: GitHub repositories, cloud service accounts, communication platforms, and any development or staging environments. Intern shall cooperate with the access revocation process.

7.4 Intern shall not share access credentials with any third party.

---

## 8. Conduct and Policies

Intern agrees to conduct themselves professionally, follow Company guidelines, and treat client information with appropriate care and discretion.

---

## 9. At-Will Arrangement

Either party may end this internship at any time with reasonable notice. If the Company terminates the internship, it shall notify WCTC promptly.

---

## 10. No Employment Relationship

This Agreement does not create an employment relationship. Intern is not entitled to employee benefits, workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance under this Agreement.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin.
