# Non-Disclosure Agreement — Intern
## Learned Geek LLC

**Entity ID:** L087476 | **EIN:** 41-3648316

---

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Learned Geek LLC, a Wisconsin Limited Liability Company ("Company"), and the undersigned intern ("Intern"), effective as of the date signed below.

---

## 1. Purpose

Intern will be participating in a software development internship with the Company. During the internship, Intern will have access to confidential and proprietary information belonging to the Company and its clients.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed to or accessed by Intern during the internship, including but not limited to: software, source code, architecture, system designs, AI prompts, business plans, client information, project scope, pricing, financial data, technical specifications, and any information designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the context.

Confidential Information explicitly includes information about the Company's clients, their business operations, and their projects, even if Intern does not interact with clients directly.

---

## 3. Obligations

Intern agrees to:

3.1 Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence.

3.2 Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party — including fellow students, family members, social media contacts, or future employers — without prior written consent from the Company.

3.3 Use Confidential Information solely for performing internship duties.

3.4 Not post, share, or reference Confidential Information on social media, personal websites, blogs, or public repositories (including public GitHub repositories).

3.5 **Portfolio Exception.** Intern may reference the general nature of the internship (e.g., "developed Blazor WebAssembly components for a healthcare technology company") in resumes and portfolios, but shall not include proprietary code, screenshots of non-public systems, client names, or project details without prior written approval from the Company.

---

## 4. Exclusions

Obligations do not apply to information that:

4.1 Is or becomes publicly known through no fault of Intern.

4.2 Was already known to Intern prior to the internship, as evidenced by written records.

4.3 Is independently developed by Intern without use of Confidential Information.

---

## 5. Return or Destruction

Upon completion or termination of the internship, Intern shall:

5.1 Return all Company materials, documents, and data in Intern's possession.

5.2 Delete all Confidential Information from personal devices, cloud storage, and any other non-Company systems.

5.3 Confirm in writing that all Confidential Information has been returned or destroyed.

---

## 6. Term

This Agreement is effective upon execution. The confidentiality obligations remain in force for two (2) years from the end of the internship.

---

## 7. Remedies

Intern acknowledges that breach of this Agreement could cause irreparable harm to the Company and its clients for which monetary damages would be inadequate. The Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to all other remedies available at law or equity.

---

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin.
