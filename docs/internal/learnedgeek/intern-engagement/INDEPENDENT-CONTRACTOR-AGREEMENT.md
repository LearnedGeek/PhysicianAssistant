# Independent Contractor Agreement
## Learned Geek LLC

**Entity ID:** L087476 | **EIN:** 41-3648316

---

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Learned Geek LLC, a Wisconsin Limited Liability Company ("Company"), and the undersigned ("Contractor"), effective as of the date signed below.

---

## 1. Services

Contractor shall perform the services described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement. Each SOW shall specify scope, deliverables, timeline, and compensation.

---

## 2. Relationship

2.1 Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, agency, or joint venture relationship.

2.2 Contractor is solely responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits. Company will not withhold taxes or provide benefits.

2.3 Contractor retains control over the manner and means of performing the services, subject to the deliverables and timelines specified in the SOW.

---

## 3. Compensation

3.1 Compensation is as specified in each SOW.

3.2 Contractor shall submit invoices as specified in the SOW. Company shall pay invoices within thirty (30) days of receipt.

3.3 Contractor is responsible for all self-employment taxes. Company will issue a Form 1099 as required by law.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

4.1 **Assignment.** Contractor hereby assigns to Learned Geek LLC all right, title, and interest in any and all work product, code, software, documentation, designs, inventions, and other materials created by Contractor in the course of performing services under this Agreement ("Work Product").

4.2 **Work Made for Hire.** To the extent permitted by law, all Work Product constitutes works made for hire belonging to the Company. To the extent any Work Product does not qualify as a work made for hire, Contractor irrevocably assigns all rights to the Company.

4.3 **Assistance.** Contractor agrees to execute any documents reasonably requested by the Company to perfect or record the Company's ownership of Work Product.

4.4 **Pre-Existing IP.** Contractor shall disclose any pre-existing IP that may be incorporated into Work Product prior to incorporation. Such pre-existing IP is not assigned under this Agreement unless separately agreed in writing. If pre-existing IP is incorporated, Contractor grants Company a perpetual, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to use it as part of the Work Product.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1 Contractor agrees to keep confidential all non-public information about the Company, its clients, projects, and technology encountered during the engagement.

5.2 This obligation survives the end of the engagement for three (3) years.

5.3 If the Parties have executed a separate NDA, the NDA governs confidentiality and supersedes this Section 5 to the extent of any conflict.

---

## 6. Data Access and Restrictions

6.1 Contractor shall access only the systems, data, and environments authorized by the Company for the specific SOW.

6.2 Contractor shall not copy, download, export, or transfer Company data to personal devices or unauthorized systems.

6.3 If the SOW involves access to personal data or protected health information, Contractor shall comply with the applicable Data Processing Agreement and any data access restrictions specified in the SOW.

---

## 7. Non-Solicitation

During the engagement and for twelve (12) months after termination, Contractor shall not directly solicit the Company's clients for competing services. This does not restrict Contractor from performing work for the same clients in unrelated areas.

---

## 8. Term and Termination

8.1 This Agreement is effective upon execution and continues until all SOWs are complete or the Agreement is terminated.

8.2 Either party may terminate with fourteen (14) days written notice.

8.3 Termination does not relieve Company of payment obligations for work completed and accepted.

8.4 Upon termination, Contractor shall return all Company materials and revoke access to Company systems within 24 hours.

8.5 IP assignment (Section 4) and Confidentiality (Section 5) survive termination indefinitely.

---

## 9. Insurance

Contractor is solely responsible for obtaining and maintaining any insurance Contractor deems necessary, including professional liability and general liability insurance.

---

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wisconsin. Disputes shall be resolved in Waukesha County, Wisconsin.

---

## 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with all executed SOWs, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior understandings.
