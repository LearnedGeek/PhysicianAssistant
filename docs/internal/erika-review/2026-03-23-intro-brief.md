## What I'm Building

I'm working with a Peruvian pediatrician named Dr. Martín Núñez (based in Lima) on an AI-powered triage assistant. The idea is that when parents need to reach the doctor after hours, instead of calling and getting no answer, they send a WhatsApp message. An AI assistant — built by me — responds, gathers symptoms, assesses urgency, and keeps the parent calm until the doctor can follow up. The doctor reviews everything through a dashboard before any clinical decision is made.

It's a real project with real commercial potential — Martin is already thinking about expanding it to other physicians in Latin America. He was at Expo West in Anaheim when we first connected, so the US market is on his radar too.
