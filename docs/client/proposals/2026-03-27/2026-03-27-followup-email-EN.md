## What We Agreed Today

1. **Data roles:** Martin is the data controller, Learned Geek is the data processor. We need a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) between both parties.

2. **ANPDP registration:** Martin registers the data bank. Learned Geek declares providers (Anthropic, Twilio) as sub-processors.

3. **International transfer:** Traceability of data flows is required. I have already reviewed the acceptable use policies of Anthropic and Twilio — no issues for healthcare use.

4. **Minor consent:** One parent or legal guardian is sufficient in practice. Consent is captured digitally in the platform.

5. **Data retention:** Records cannot be deleted — only blocked. The system preserves raw data without modification. Full traceability.

6. **DIGEMID classification:** We are in a gray area. Not a blocker for the MVP, but there is long-term strategic benefit in voluntary registration. We continue evaluating.

7. **Clinical responsibility:** The AI generates recommendations; the physician maintains clinical responsibility. The physician's VoBo (Visto Bueno) validates all information.
