## 10. Vesting

All equity vests over **24 months** with a **6-month cliff**. Neither partner receives their full equity stake immediately. This ensures both partners remain committed through the critical early phase.

**How this works in practice:** Equity is earned gradually over 24 months of active participation — each month, 1/24th of your total stake is earned. The "cliff" means that for the first 6 months, no equity has vested yet. At month 6, the first 25% vests all at once. After that, it continues monthly. If either partner exits before the cliff (6 months), they leave with no equity. This is standard in technology partnerships worldwide — it protects both partners by ensuring that the person who stays and does the work is the one who owns the equity.
